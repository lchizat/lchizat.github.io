Lénaïc Chizat

I will be joining EPFL in September 2021 and I am looking for post-docs and PhD students (see Announcements).

I am currently a CNRS researcher at Laboratoire de mathématiques d'Orsay at Université Paris-Saclay. In 2018, I was a post-doc researcher at INRIA Paris working with F. Bach. In 2017, I have completed a PhD in applied mathematics at Université Paris-Dauphine (CEREMADE) and École Normale Supérieure (DMA) working with G. Peyré and F-X. Vialard.

I am interested in the mathematical analysis of data-driven algorithms. More specifically I have worked on the following topics:

  • optimal transport and variational problems in the space of measures

  • continuous optimization algorithms (convex and non-convex)

  • artificial neural networks (training and generalization)
