PhD thesis

I defended my PhD thesis entitled Unbalanced Optimal Transport: Models, Numerical Methods, Applications, at Université Paris-Dauphine (PSL Research university) on November 10th 2017. My thesis advisors were G. Peyré and F-X. Vialard.

  • slides of the defense (in French, clickable animations require Acrobat Reader)

  • PhD manuscript (in English)

Research interests

  • optimal transport and variational problems in the space of measures

  • continuous optimization algorithms (convex and non-convex)

  • applications to statistical learning and signal processing
